I'm writing a series of posts about the story of [Moona](https://getmoona.com), the start-up company I've been working on since November 2016.

I will tell the story of our company, our challenges, our mission and how we got from an initial prototype to a shipped product, from the moment I joined, which was 6 months after the project creation, up until now, 3 years later.

This series will include 7 articles after this one, with a focus on product development and technical challenges specific to the hardware-software mix of our venture, but also on team creation and management:

1. Going to CES
2. HAX Shenzhen
3. Kickstarting
4. New Beginning (or how to actually start)
5. "Making it"
6. "Really making it"
7. Shipping

Hope you enjoy reading it, and if you find it helpful in any way and want to know more, don't hesitate to drop a line in the comments section of the posts.
